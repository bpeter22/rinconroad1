Rush Orders

Rush production and expedited shipping are available according to the following fee schedule and subject to availability. Normal production time is usually 2-3 business days. Note: Rush charges are calculated from the total cost of your order before shipping, and do not affect time in transit with the shipping courier.

  • For 1/2 the normal production time, the fee is an additional 100% of your order.
  • For 1/4 the normal production time, the fee is an additional 200% of your order.
  • Separately, expedited shipping (including overnight service) is available and billed at cost on your Final Invoice.

Additional payment for rush production service or expedited shipping service may be due after checkout subject to our Terms of Sale, Service, and Use. To arrange a rush order, please Contact Us.
