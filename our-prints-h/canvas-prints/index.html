Quality materials

We use museum-quality canvas, giving you a vibrant and durable print. Prints are wrapped around an artist's stretcher frame and finished ready-to-hang with hardware.

Professional printing

We print the image directly onto museum-quality canvas material using high-quality archival inks. A lustre coating is applied to each piece to protect the print surface.

Shipping

Your Rincon Road canvas print will be professionally printed, packed, and delivered to your home ready-to-hang. We offer affordable flat rate shipping options starting at just $6.00. Following receipt of your Final Invoice and payment in full, production time is approximately 2-3 business days. Standard shipping speed is 5-10 days depending on location, and expedited shipping is available for an additional fee. Our professional printer consistently delivers high-quality prints quickly, however, exact delivery dates cannot be guaranteed.

Rush Orders

Rush production and expedited shipping are available according to the following fee schedule. Rush charges are calculated from the total cost of your order before shipping, and do not affect time in transit. For 1/2 the normal production time, the fee is an additional 100% of your order. For 1/4 the normal production time, the fee is an additional 200% of your order. Separately, expedited shipping (including overnight service) is available and billed at cost on your Final Invoice. Additional payment for rush production service or expedited shipping service may be due after checkout subject to our Terms of Sale, Service, and Use.
